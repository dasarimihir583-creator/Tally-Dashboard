# Professional Audit & Accounting Toolkit

Developed by **dasarimihir583-creator**

A collection of high-efficiency audit tools, tax compliance engines, and data visualization dashboards designed for Chartered Accountants and finance professionals. This repository bridges the gap between complex tax regulations and automated digital solutions.

## 🛠️ Tool Directory

### 1. GST Compliance Engines
* **Place of Supply (POS) Logic Engine**
    * A web-based interactive tool to determine whether a transaction attracts IGST or CGST/SGST based on the IGST Act, 2017. Features scenarios for both goods and services, including B2B/B2C logic.
* **Value of Supply (VOS) Engine**
    * Calculates taxable value based on Section 15 and Chapter IV Valuation Rules. Covers specific scenarios like air travel agents, life insurance, and second-hand goods margin schemes.
* **Time of Supply (TOS) Logic Engine**
    * Determines the point in time when the liability to pay GST arises under Section 12 & 13 of the CGST Act. Includes logic for reverse charge mechanisms and voucher redemption.

### 2. Audit & Data Visualization
* **Tally-Dashboard (Power BI)**
    * A custom Power BI dashboard designed to visualize and scrutinize Tally data. Provides an automated workflow for ledger analysis and client turnover insights.

## 🚀 Why these tools?
These tools are built for:
* **Automation:** Reducing manual tax calculations and error rates in audit working papers.
* **Compliance:** Ensuring adherence to the latest GST regulations through algorithmic logic.
* **Efficiency:** Providing a "single-click" solution for complex multi-step audit processes.

## 💻 Tech Stack
* **Web Interfaces:** HTML5, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript (Vanilla).
* **Data Analysis:** Power BI (DAX & M-Query integration).
* **Compliance Logic:** Based on the Income Tax Act & GST Regulations, 2017.
